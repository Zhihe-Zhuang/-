About me

I was born in February 17th, 1997 in Suqian, Jiangsu Province, China. In June 2019, I received the B.Eng. degree with a major in Automation at the School of Internet of Things Engineering, Jiangnan University in Wuxi, China.

In September 2019, I began my Ph.D. studies at Jiangnan University under the supervision of Prof. Hongfeng Tao. My main interests are in the areas of iterative learning control, optimization, and reinforcement learning. I expect to graduate in June of 2025.
